Udon Carbonara

Home
Creamy carbonary mixed with ham topped with parmesan

Ingredients

  • Udon noodles
  • Ham
  • All purpose cream
  • Beef cube
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Margarine

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles for 7-10 minutes
  2. Fry the ham in oil and slice the ham
  3. Prepare the garlic and onions
  4. Mince the garlic and slice the onion then toss the garlic and onions in the pan. Fry the onion and garlic in margarine
  5. Let it fry for 3-5 minutes
  6. Add cream and Beef cube and let them simmer. Then season with salt and pepper
  7. Add the udon and parmesan in the pan and let it simmer
  8. Then serve
